Values

What we care about most

Be Decisive

We encourage diverse viewpoints, engage in open dialogue, and crystallize our thoughts in writing. Once a decision is made, we rally together in unity.

Expect Greatness

As a team, we thrive on challenges and mutual support. We’re firm believers in the talent and ambition that resides within each individual, driving them to excel in their careers and advance our shared mission.

Cultivate Growth

We actively pursue smart, deliberate growth. We believe continuous learning and mentorship are fundamental to success. We nurture each other’s ambitions.

Lead with Empathy

We prioritize a culture of kindness, compassion, and respect. We embrace obstacles and work together to navigate the best path.

Innovate

We push the boundaries of what’s possible in the radiographic industry. We focus on solving providers’ and patients’ problems.
